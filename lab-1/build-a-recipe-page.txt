  <h1>Making Tea</h1>
  <p>To make chai, boil water with tea, ginger, and spices. Then add milk and sugar, let it simmer for a few minutes, and strain it into your cup.
  </br>Your delicious, warm chai is ready to drink!
  </p>
  <h2>Ingredients</h2>
  <ul>
    <li>Water</li>
    <li>Milk</li>
    <li>Sugar</li>
    <li>Tea</li>
  </ul>
  <h2>Instructions</h2>
  <ol>
    <li>2 cups of water</li>
    <li>2 cups of milk (any kind you like)</li>
    <li>4 teaspoons of loose black tea leaves (or 4 tea bags)

</li>
    <li>4 teaspoons of sugar (add more or less as you like)

</li>
  </ol>
